AI + Research

How to Find Research as a High Schooler

What is research? Perhaps you have heard of high school students participating in such an amazing activity. You too can be involved. To start, make a resume- an example can be found here. Then, you can start sending cold emails out to university professors. Suprisingly, faculty at many great school like Wayne State, Oakland University, and Michigan State University offer opportunities to high school students! Usually, the best time to reach out is around March-April, as this time of year professors are looking for new students for the summer.

Simply search for the field that interests you, then click on professors in each field. Usually, you may need to send tens to hundreds of emails to get an acceptance. Good luck!

Coding

Learn Coding For Free

Want to systematically learn code so you can build your own coding projects? Harvard's CS50 Introduction to Programming with Python includes everything you will need to get started. It's full package of Python's interactive enviroment will allow you to start coding. If you're looking for more, you can check out CS50 Introduction to Computer Science where you can learn softwares ranging from SQL to HTML/CSS to even Cybersecurity.

Free Northville High School Computer Science Tutoring

Having trouble with a computer science class and not sure what to do? Email sclatech@northvilleschools.org to recieve free peer coding tutoring! We meet weekly in Ms. Sclater's room 206. All are welcome!
