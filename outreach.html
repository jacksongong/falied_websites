Math

NHS Math Tutoring

Interested in volunteer hours? Want to help your peers with math? Sign up to teach at Northville Peer Math Tutoring! We meet biweekly in Mr. Kinney's room 242.
You will recieve both national honors society hours as well as a certificate at the end of the year!

MATHCOUNTS Tutoring

Looking for an opportunity to teach competitive math? Email gongji@northvilleschools.net to teach at Northville Meads Mill and Hillside middle schools!
You will recieve presidential service hours for your teaching!

Mathnasium & NCCAE

Want to teach math out of school on a consistant basis? Mathnasium is a Paid, easily accessible tutoring opportunity for all students. Call 877-601-6284 for more information or visit their website NCCAE is another oranization aimed to help the younger generation with STEM. Check out their website here .
